Introduction

This page is about the tennis major championships for Mens Singles.

Tennis is a sport where two players hit a small rubber ball over a net back and forth across a court until one player 'wins' the point

There are three main surfaces:

  • Hard Court - This is a concrete based court. Both the US Open and Australian Open are played on hard court.
  • Clay Surface - This court surface is clay based. The French Open is played on clay.
  • Grass Surface - This court surface is grass based. Wimbledon is played on grass.
